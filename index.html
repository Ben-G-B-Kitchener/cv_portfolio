Profile

Hi! I'm a Senior Electronics Engineer working for UK Atomic Energy Authority (UKAEA) based at UKAEA South Yorkshire in Rotherham, providing instrumentation, electronics, software, consultancy, and commissioning support to fusion technology projects.

I was Lead C&I Engineer for the ELSA project, delivering a cryogenic test facility supporting STEP Re-mountable Joints and other fusion research programmes.

My background includes R&D in fusion, nuclear (Rolls Royce), particle physics (CERN), aerospace, environmental systems, and industrial instrumentation development.
